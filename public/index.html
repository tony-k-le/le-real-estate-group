Property overview

This thoughtfully updated 2-bedroom, 1-bath duplex (front unit) blends classic Long Beach charm with modern upgrades, featuring an airy open layout, restored mahogany wood floors, and a stylish kitchen complete with granite surfaces and stainless steel appliances. The oversized front yard sets this home apart — perfect for outdoor entertaining, gardening, or weekend projects. Every detail, from designer paint to uniform 2" blinds, creates an easy-to-love, move-in-ready experience.

Positioned near CSULB with seamless 405 access, the home offers a rare balance of suburban calm and urban convenience: grab coffee on the way to campus or work, bike to the beach, or head downtown in minutes for dining and nightlife.
